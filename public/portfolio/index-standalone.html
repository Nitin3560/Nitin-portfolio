UAV Swarm Coordination Simulator

Fault Tolerant Multi Agent Control Architecture · Python · PyBullet · CTDE MAPPO

Thesis driven simulation platform for resilient multi UAV coordination under GPS drift, communication degradation, and sensor corruption. The system combines a cascaded control loop with a supervisory multi agent layer to study how autonomous teams recover gracefully when the environment stops cooperating. The broader research thread also connects swarm control with low altitude wireless network behavior, communication aware coordination, and edge supervision. Specific quantitative results are intentionally omitted here while the manuscript is under review.

I'm an MS Computer Science student at UT Arlington (graduating Dec 2026) with 2+ years of professional backend engineering under my belt, and I'm most interested in systems that have to keep working when things go wrong.

My research focuses on UAV swarm coordination and fault tolerant control systems, building multi agent architectures that maintain mission integrity under GPS drift, signal degradation, and sensor corruption.
